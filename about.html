F.A.Q.

Q1: Where are you based?

I reside in a suburban town in East London, England. It is close enough to Central London whilst maintaining a quaint village feeling.

Q2: Do you have any budget travel recommendations?

From students to parents to busy professionals, there are always reasons that people feel they can't make their globetrotting dreams come true, and it usually is becuase of the price tag. I recommend Hopper App for deals when they arise, Skyscanner to compare cheap flights and checking the airlines' low far calender!

Q3: How do you take your photos?

I use a Canon 5D Mark III, with a mixture of lenses including the Canon 24-70mm. I also use a Manfrotto 3-section tripod for flatylays and stop-motions. Occasionally I use the iPhone 8 Plus for other images!.
